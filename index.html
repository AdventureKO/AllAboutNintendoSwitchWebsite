ALL ABOUT NINTENDO SWITCH









What Is The Nintendo Switch?

The Nintendo Switch is a hybrid video game console that can play games on the go or connected to a TV or other device at 1080p resolution. It is a platform that carries an abundance of excellent first party Nintendo titles and great third party games too.




What Is The Nintendo Switch Lite?

The Nintendo Switch Lite is a cheaper, portable only console that can play most games on the original Nintendo Switch on a smaller screen.




Trivial Information About Consoles

  • The Nintendo Switch was released early 2017

  • The Nintendo Switch Lite was released late 2019

  • There are rumors of a Nintendo Switch Pro, coming with full 4K resolution, faster processing chip, and other game-changing improvements

  • Total hardware sales: 79.87 Million Units as of April 2021

  • Total software sales: 532.34 Million Units as of April 2021

  • The Nintendo Switch is the successor to the Wii U

  • The Nintendo Switch was originally referred to as Nintendo NX during development

  • During the Covid-19 Pandemic, Nintendo Switch sales have skyrocketed as more people picked up or made the switch to Nintendo Switch for entertainment

  • Nintendo releases new content upcoming to the Nintendo Switch in Nintendo Direct livestreams and E3 Presentations
